Take Shape For Life® Elevate Now Events


Elevate Now Events are coming to your community! Take Shape For Life® is excited to announce that Health Coaches across the country will be holding events to elevate your Optimal Wellbeing™ on Saturday, January 7th, 2017.

The goal is to reach as many people as possible, and help everyone to understand the importance of healthy living! This is an opportunity for Health Coaches to welcome a few new members into our Optimal Wellbeing™ Community, and elevate their potential in 2017!

We’d love for all of our Health Coaches to join in this celebration, and be a part of this nationwide event! There are two ways to participate in this year’s Elevate Now Events. You can either host an event in your area, or choose to attend a fellow Health Coach’s event. Whether your event has 2 attendees or 200, we encourage everyone to get involved and help us share the message of healthy living and Optimal Wellbeing™ !
